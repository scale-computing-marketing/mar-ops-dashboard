SC//Operations · Marketing Stack

Technology Overview — the stack

The platforms that power Scale Computing's marketing operations — what each tool does and why it earns a seat in the stack.

Platforms 5 tools

Salesloft
Sales Enablement

Streamlines the sales process with AI-powered insights, automated lead nurturing, and accelerated sales cycles. Tools for personalized buyer engagement, sales forecasting, and pipeline management help teams prioritize high-value opportunities. Real-time analytics improve deal closure rates and drive customer retention and expansion — helping sales operate more efficiently, close more deals, and grow revenue.

Demandbase
Firmographic Enrichment

Helps B2B teams identify, target, and engage the right accounts using AI-powered insights and data integration. The account-based marketing platform enables personalized outreach, faster sales velocity, and higher conversion rates. Demandbase aligns sales and marketing, automates tasks, and provides real-time analytics — integrating cleanly with the broader stack to optimize advertising, CRM, and sales efforts.

Pardot
Marketing Automation

Streamlines lead generation, nurturing, and segmentation with automated, personalized campaigns. Integrates natively with Salesforce, offers in-depth reporting, and improves ROI through targeted marketing. Automation and scalability boost efficiency, cross-team collaboration, and overall campaign performance.

Qualified
Website Chat

Engages website visitors in real time through AI-powered conversations, live chat, and intelligent routing. Qualified helps surface high-intent buyers, personalize web experiences, and accelerate pipeline generation. It streamlines lead qualification by connecting prospects directly with sales reps, automating meeting scheduling, and delivering actionable insights into buyer behavior — with seamless CRM and marketing automation integration.

BeePro
Email Builder

A no-code platform for creating responsive emails and landing pages. Beefree offers an intuitive drag-and-drop editor, more than 1,500 customizable templates, and mobile-responsive designs. Real-time collaboration, integrations with tools like Mailchimp and Salesforce, and reusable content blocks save time — a strong fit for marketing teams that want to move quickly without engineering support.
